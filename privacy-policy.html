Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. Streaksmith is designed to be completely private and secure.

Data Collection

We collect ZERO data.

Streaksmith does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no analytics, no tracking, no servers, and no third-party services.

Data Storage

All habit data is stored locally on your device only. Your data never leaves your device.

  • Habits and completion history: Stored locally using Hive database
  • App settings and preferences: Stored locally on device
  • No cloud backup or sync
  • No user accounts or authentication

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are processed locally on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Permissions

Streaksmith may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you daily habit reminders (optional)
  • Storage: To save your habit data locally on your device

Third-Party Services

Streaksmith does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Streaksmith does not knowingly collect any information from anyone. The app is safe for all ages.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Data" option in app settings
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
